FAQS

What is a privacy shutter on a laptop?

A privacy shutter on a laptop is a physical cover for the webcam that can be slid open or closed to block or allow the camera's view.

Why are laptops with privacy shutters becoming popular?

Laptops with privacy shutters are becoming popular due to increasing concerns about privacy and cybersecurity, especially with the rise in remote work and online meetings.

Do all laptops come with a privacy shutter?

No, not all laptops come with a privacy shutter. It is a feature that is specifically included in some laptop models to provide users with added control over their webcam.

How does a privacy shutter work?

A privacy shutter typically slides over the webcam lens to physically block it when not in use, ensuring that the camera is not inadvertently activated without the user's knowledge.

Are there any drawbacks to using a laptop with a privacy shutter?

One possible drawback of using a laptop with a privacy shutter is that it may add a small amount of thickness to the device due to the mechanism, but this is usually minimal and outweighed by the added privacy protection.
