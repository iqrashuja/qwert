Despite being powerful tools that can enhance classroom learning, laptops could potentially lead to a decrease in focus and engagement. With plenty of possibilities at their fingertips, students may easily get sidetracked by non-educational content such as social media or games. Additionally, the compact dimensions and lightweight design of modern laptops, such as the ASUS laptops for teachers, make it easy to carry them around, raising concerns about misuse during classes.

Another drawback of using laptops in classrooms is the issue of technical limitations. For instance, the ASUS laptops for teachers, equipped with Intel Core processors, can provide efficient performance for educators.
