How Microsoft Surface laptops handle accidental spills

Microsoft Surface laptops are designed with a focus on durability and resilience, including protection against accidental spills. The keyboards of these laptops are sealed to prevent liquid penetration into the internal components, minimizing the potential damage caused by spills. In addition to the sealed keyboard, the touchpad area is also designed to be resistant to liquid ingress, providing an extra layer of protection against spills that may occur during day-to-day use.

Furthermore, Microsoft has integrated a sensor in some Surface models that can detect liquid contact. When a spill is detected, the laptop immediately shuts down to prevent any further damage. This automatic shutdown feature helps to safeguard the laptop from potential short circuits or other electrical issues that may arise from liquid exposure.
